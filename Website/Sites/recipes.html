Freddo Espresso

ðŸ‡¬ðŸ‡·

Cold and lightly sweet double espresso. Delicious during hot summer months.

Required materials:

7-8 ice cubes, double espresso, sugar, milk frother

Recipe:

Brew a double shot of espresso out of your preferred coffee machine.
Add sugar and one ice cube to the coffee (1 tsp - light / 2tsp - medium / 2tsp - sweet) and froth up until the coffee gets foamy.
Fill your glass with ice only (no water!).
Then, add the coffee mixture to the glass.
Enjoy!

Iced Americano

ðŸ‡ºðŸ‡¸

The original americano experience during hot days.

Required materials:

8-9 ice cubes, double espresso, sugar (if preferred), cold water

Recipe:

Brew a double shot of espresso out of your preferred coffee machine.
If you like add sugar to the coffee and one ice cube to cool it down.
Fill your glass with ice cubes.
Then, add the coffee to the glass.
Fill up your glass with cold water.
Enjoy and never give up!

Latte Macchiato

ðŸ‡®ðŸ‡¹

One of the classics with hot milk.

Required materials:

milk, single espresso, sugar (if preferred), milk frother

Recipe:

Brew a single shot of espresso out of your preferred coffee machine.
Froth 175ml of milk.
Fill your glass with the frothed milk.
Then, add the shot of espresso to the glass.
If preferred, add sugar.
Enjoy!

Iced Latte

ðŸŒŽ

Milky and light iced coffee. Delicious during summer and perfectly customizable.

Required materials:

9-10 ice cubes, single or double espresso, sugar (if preferred), milk, milk frother

Recipe:

Brew a single or double shot of espresso out of your preferred coffee machine.
Add preferred amount of sugar and one ice cube to the coffee.
Froth up your cold milk.
Add your cold milk to a larger glass filled with ice cubes.
Add your cooled shot of coffee on top.
Enjoy!
