Greek Frappe

ðŸ‡¬ðŸ‡·

The most popular coffee of Greece.

Required materials:

7-8 ice cubes, instant coffee, sugar, milk frother, cold water

Recipe:

Put your instant coffee and sugar into a glass with ratio: (1:1 -> light / 1:1.5 -> medium / 1:2 -> sweet).
Add only a little bit of water so it covers the sugar and coffee (by about a finger-height more water).
Froth it up until a very smooth, light brown foam forms up.
Then, add the ice cubes and a bit of water in your glass.
Enjoy!

For those who love ice cream and coffee!

Required materials:

vanilla ice cream, espresso

Recipe:

Brew a double or single shot of espresso out of your preferred coffee machine.
Let it cool down for a second
Fill your glass with ice cream, leave a bit of space on top for the coffee.
Then, add the coffee on top of the ice cream.
Enjoy!

Vietnamese Iced Coffee

ðŸ‡»ðŸ‡³

One of the classics with hot milk.

Required materials:

sweetened condensed milk, ground coffee, hot water, 7-8 ice cubes

Recipe:

Brew coffee using the drip style. Put 3 tablespoons of coffee in your filter.
Add 20ml of hot water.
After waiting for a little, add another 80ml of hot water.
Then, add the fully dripped coffee into a glass.
Add at least 2 teaspoons of sweetened condensed milk and mix.
Add ice cubes to your mixture.
Enjoy!
